Emergence

You write to your spouse:

My love,
My team and I have discovered a strange asteroid field out in the depths of the Third System.
On every asteroid in the field is a portal to another asteroid.
The mining oppurtunities here are endless.
We can only manage to run 5 factories on each asteroid, any more and it becomes unstable.
However, an enemy of the state has also come across this field.
The government has ordered me to produce robotic tanks in my mining factories to help destroy the enemy.
But the nearest government agent is lightyears away, so it is up to me to defeat the enemy and gain control for the government.

Controls


Arrow Keys- Move around
Click- Build
Drag- Select units
Right Click-Direct units. You may direct them to another planet.
Scroll- Zoom
M- Mute music
F11- Fullscreen
The more factories you have, the faster you earn money.
Bullets do more damage if they are closer to their target.
Factories are $5 000
Tanks are $2 000
